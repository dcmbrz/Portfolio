More About Me

Greetings! My name is Michael Ekwueme and I am currently a fourth-year at the University of California Irvine pursuing my bachelors degree in Public Health Policy with a minor in Health Informatics.

I'm an energetic, optimistic, and self-motivated young professional eagerly anticipating the unfolding of my future endeavors. With a foundation in Public Health Policy, coupled with a deep-seated affinity for computer science and AI, my focus lies in revolutionizing healthcare accessibility, particularly for those in low-income and marginalized communities.

My passion for utilizing technology to address societal challenges drives me to constantly seek new avenues for growth and knowledge acquisition within the realms of computer science, healthcare, and AI. I am committed to exploring innovative solutions that not only improve healthcare outcomes but also empower individuals from all walks of life to lead healthier, more fulfilling lives. Ready to embark on a journey of exploration, innovation, and impact, I eagerly await the opportunity to contribute positively to the intersection of these dynamic fields.
